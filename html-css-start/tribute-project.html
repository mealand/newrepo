Nikola Tesla

Life and times of Nikola Tesla

Early years:

  • Nikola Tesla was born on the 10 July 1856
  • He was born an ethnic Serb in the village fo Smiljan, Lika county, in the Austrian Empire, present day Crotia
  • His father, Milutin Tesla was an Eastern Orthodox Priest, while his mother ƒêuka Tesla made home craft tools and mechanical appliances
  • Tesla had four siblings all together,three sisters and one elder brother
  • Yound Tesla attended primary school in Smiljan where he studied German, arithmetic and religion
  • He attended high school at the Higher Real Gymnasium in the year 1870

Education:

  • In 1861, Tesla attended primary school in Smiljan, where he studied German, arithemetic, and religion.
  • In 1862, His family moved to Gospic Lika, where he completed primary and middle school
  • In 1870, Tesla moved to Karlovac to attend High school at the Higher Real Gymnasium, where he finished a four-year term in three years, graduating in 1873.
  • In 1875, Tesla enrolled at Austrian Polytechnic in Graz, Austria, on a Military Frontier scholarship, but he dropped out and never graduated from the university.
  • In 1880, he attended lectures in philosophy at Charles-Ferdinand university as an auditor but he did not receive grades for the courses because he was not formally enrolled.

Personal Life:

  • Tesla was 6 feet 2 inches (1.88 m) tall and weighed 142 pounds (64 kg), he had light eyes, very big hands, and remarkably big thumbs.
  • Tesla could memorize complete books, he had photographic memory, he was also a polygot speaking eight languages: Serbo-Croatian, Czech, English, French, German, Hungarian, Italian, and Latin.
  • Tesla could visualize an invention in his mind with extreme precision, including all dimensions, before moving to the construction stage, a technique sometimes known as picture thinking.
  • Tesla claimed never to sleep more than two hours per night. However, he did admit to dozing from time to time to recharge his batteries.
  • He could play billards, chess or cards for 48 hours or more at a stretch.
  • For exercise, Tesla walked between 8 and 10 miles (13 and 16 km) per day. He curled his toes one hundred times for each foot every night, saying that it stimulated his brain cells.
  • Tesla became a vegetarian in his later years, living on only milk, bread, honey, and vegetable juices.
  • Tesla never married explaining that his chastity was very helpful to his scientific abilities.

Awards:

  • Serbia, 1892: Grand Officer of the Order of St. Sava.
  • Franklin Institute, USA, 1894: Elliott Cresson Medal.
  • Montenegro, 1895: Grand Cross of the Order of Prince Danilo I.
  • Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers, USA, 1917: AIEE Edison Medal.

Death:

  • In 1937 at 81 years Nikola Tesla had an accident which severely affected his back and ribs.
  • He never recovered from the accident. He died in 1943 at age 86
