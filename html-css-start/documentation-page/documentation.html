Skills Intro

It is interesting to know the great wonders of the human mind. So you have spent years studying and training to be a professional in one particular field of your choosing. Now you have graduated and you have been introduced into the labour market where you will toil and toil for at least one decade before retiring to the sumber anticipation of death.
Life doesn't have to be this way. You are on top of the ecosystem's food chain for a reason. You mind has the capacity to learn and be a professional at multiple fields. So you can be a:

  • Doctor and a Lawyer.
  • Architect and a Programmer
  • Musician and a Politician
  • Footballer and a Photographer
Basically the choice is yours. You can either choose to make your life interesting by learning new things and be a professional in multiple fields, or you can just follow the majority that live life in the direction of the wind.
Regarding my admonishment, this document describes the several skills I have acquired over the years.

This love for music has driven me to learn and master how to:

  • Play the piano

  • Play drums
  • Use my voice as an instrument for singing
  • Read music
There so many more instrument I'll love to learn and perfect and I will do that as soon as time permits me.
